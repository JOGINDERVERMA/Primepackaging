About Our Factory

Pioneering Advanced Woven Bag Manufacturing in Sardarshahar, Rajasthan

Established at Bikaner road, behind nayara petrol pump, Bandhanu Dikhnada, Sardarshahar (Rajasthan 331403), Prime Packaging is a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant dedicated to producing ultra-durable Polypropylene (PP) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) woven sacks, BOPP multicolor bags, and customized industrial packaging.

With over 1,50,000 sq. ft. of modern plant space and high-speed Starlinger and Lohia tape extrusion lines and circular looms, we guarantee uncompromised burst strength, zero-defect weaving, and brilliant food-grade packaging that protects your valuable commodities across all climatic conditions.

Step-by-Step Workflow

Manufacturing Process Timeline

Precision engineering from virgin polymer granules to final baled dispatch at our Rajasthan plant.

Step 01
Raw Material

Virgin PP & HDPE granules inspection and precise masterbatch dosing.

Step 02
Extrusion

High-speed tape lines stretching high-tensile, uniform flat yarns.

Step 03
Weaving

6 & 8 shuttle circular looms weaving seamless, high-strength fabric tubes.

Step 04
Lamination

BOPP film & PE extrusion coating ensuring 100% moisture barrier.

Step 05
Printing

Up to 8-color HD rotogravure & roll-to-roll flexo precision printing.

Step 06
Cutting

Automated heat & ultrasonic cutting to prevent any thread fraying.

Step 07
Stitching

Heavy-duty double-fold bottom & top hem industrial stitching.

Step 08
Inspection

Drop test, tensile breaking strength, and strict zero-defect lab checks.

Step 09
Bale Packing

Hydraulic bale pressing into tight, secure bales of 500/1000 bags.

Step 10
Rapid Dispatch

Direct factory logistics from Sardarshahar across all 20+ states in India.

Rigorous Quality Assurance Protocols

Every batch produced at our Sardarshahar facility undergoes a strict 6-stage quality inspection before it is certified for dispatch. We adhere to IS 11652 / IS 14887 Indian Bureau of Standards and international export standards.

Request Quality Test Reports
Strength & Drop Test

Loaded bags undergo repeated free-fall drop tests from 1.5m height to verify zero seam splitting.

Weight & GSM Test

Precision digital weighing checks verify exact tape denier and fabric grams per square meter (GSM).

Size Inspection

Automated optical measurement ensures exact length, width, and gusset folding dimensions.

Color & Gloss Matching

Spectrophotometer checks ensure exact Pantone shades and high gloss BOPP film brightness.

Printing Adhesion Quality

Tape peel tests check ink adhesion durability so branding never rubs off during transport.

Final Bale QC & Count

Hydraulic baling verification ensures exact bag counts per bale with moisture-proof protective wrapping.

For standard unprinted PP woven bags, our MOQ is 3,000 bags. For custom 4-color flexo printed or 8-color BOPP multicolor photo-laminated bags, our MOQ is 5,000 bags per design size to optimize printing cylinder setup costs.

Polypropylene (PP) bags offer higher stiffness, clearer printing brightness, and excellent heat resistance, making them ideal for food grains, sugar, and flour. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) bags offer superior cold flexibility, higher impact toughness, and chemical resistance, making them preferred for fertilizers, salts, and industrial chemicals.

Yes, absolutely! We manufacture completely tailor-made bags ranging from 55 GSM light-duty up to 140+ GSM extra heavy-duty, with widths between 14 inches to 36 inches and any customized length required for your product density.

Yes, we supply bags with loosely inserted or bottom-sewn food-grade virgin LDPE/LLDPE inner liners (from 25 to 60 microns) to provide a 100% waterproof and airtight barrier for sugar, flour, chemicals, and hygroscopic fertilizers.

Flexographic printing applies ink directly onto the woven fabric (up to 4 colors), ideal for simple logos and bulk transport. BOPP lamination involves reverse printing on a crystal-clear polypropylene film using rotogravure cylinders (up to 8 colors) and laminating it onto the bag, delivering photographic, scratch-proof, glossy/matte retail packaging that dominates store shelves.

Yes, we use 100% virgin food-grade polypropylene and polyethylene granules certified by FSSAI and international food safety standards, ensuring zero toxicity or odor transfer to rice, wheat, pulses, and sugar.

Yes, we manufacture retail bags equipped with die-cut D-handles or heavy-duty sewn loop handles (for 5kg-25kg sacks), as well as AD-STAR block bottom valve bags for automated cement and chemical filling nozzles.

For standard sizes or repeat orders, dispatch occurs within 5 to 7 days. For new custom BOPP printed orders requiring cylinder engraving and approval, production and dispatch take 10 to 14 days.

Yes! We send free physical fabric and sample bags via courier anywhere in India so your quality engineers can test the GSM, stitch quality, and drop-impact strength before confirming your bulk commercial order.

All finished bags are compressed using high-pressure hydraulic balers and wrapped in protective PP fabric covers with heavy steel/plastic strapping in bundles of 500 or 1,000 bags, keeping freight volume compact and preventing moisture/dust ingress during transit.

Yes, upon request, we dose specialized UV stabilizer masterbatch additives (e.g. 200 to 1600 KLY) into the polymer mix to protect bags stored under direct sunlight for 6 to 18 months without degradation or brittleness.
